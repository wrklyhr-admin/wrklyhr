## 1.1 What is wrkly.hr — In Plain English

wrkly.hr is a service where a **tech professional pays a monthly fee**, and **our team finds them contract or hourly remote work** at early-stage startups in the US and EU.

The user does nothing after signing up. We do the searching, applying, and outreach on their behalf.

That's the whole idea. No buzzwords. No complexity.

---

## 1.2 Who This is For

### The Talent (our paying customer)

```
Who they are:
  - Software Engineers (SDE) — frontend, backend, fullstack
  - DevOps / Cloud Engineers — AWS, GCP, Azure, CI/CD, Kubernetes

Where they are:
  - Based in India (primary)
  - English-speaking
  - Looking for remote work at foreign startups

What they want:
  - Earn in USD/EUR while sitting in India
  - Contract or hourly work — not a 9-5 job
  - Someone else to handle the job hunt

What they hate:
  - Spending hours on job boards
  - Ghosting after applying
  - Not knowing which startups are hiring
```

### The Startup (who we reach out to — not a paying user in MVP)

```
Who they are:
  - Early-stage tech startups (Seed to Series B)
  - Based in US or EU
  - Hiring remotely

What they want:
  - A reliable engineer or devops person fast
  - Flexible contract/hourly — not a full-time hire
  - Someone already vetted and available

What they hate:
  - Spending months recruiting
  - Expensive recruitment agencies
  - Unresponsive candidates
```

## 1.3 How the Business Works — Step by Step

```
TALENT SIDE                          OUR TEAM                        STARTUP SIDE
-----------                          --------                         ------------

1. Talent signs up on wrkly.hr
                                  
2. Talent picks a plan (Free/Paid)

3. Talent builds their profile
   - Skills, experience, rate
   - Resume upload (PDF)
   - Availability & timezone

4. Talent submits & waits
                                  5. Our team reviews new profiles

                                  6. Our team searches for recently
                                     funded startups actively hiring
                                     Sources:
                                     - Crunchbase
                                     - Y Combinator
                                     - AngelList
                                     - LinkedIn Jobs
                                     - Sequoia portfolio

                                  7. Our team reaches out to startups
                                     with a curated pitch + profile

                                                                   8. Startup responds with interest

                                  9. Our team notifies the talent
                                     "You have an interview at XYZ"

10. Talent interviews & gets placed

11. Talent works on contract/hourly
    - Gets paid directly by startup
    - wrkly.hr earns placement fee
    - Talent stays subscribed
```

## 1.5 Revenue Streams

### Stream 1 — Talent Subscriptions (Primary, MVP)

```
Month 3 Target:
  - 100 total signups
  - 75 on Free plan
  - 25 on paid plans

Paid plan breakdown (assumed):
  - 15 × Starter  (₹499)  = ₹7,485/mo
  - 7  × Growth   (₹999)  = ₹6,993/mo
  - 3  × Pro      (₹1999) = ₹5,997/mo
                            ─────────────
  Total MRR (Month 3):      ₹20,475/mo (~$245 USD)
```

### Stream 2 — Startup Placement Fee (Phase 2, not MVP)

```
When a talent gets placed:
  - We charge the startup 10% of the first month's contract value
  
  Example:
    Talent earns $3,000 for first month
    wrkly.hr earns $300 placement fee
    
  Note: Do NOT build billing for this in MVP.
        Log placements manually. Invoice startups manually.
```

## 1.6 What Our Team Does Every Day

This is important for the admin dashboard design to make sense.

```
DAILY OPERATIONS — wrkly.hr Internal Team

Morning:
  1. Check new talent signups overnight
  2. Review & approve new profiles
  3. Check application status updates from startups

Midday:
  4. Source new startups from Crunchbase, YC, LinkedIn
  5. Add startups to internal CRM
  6. Draft outreach emails to startups with talent profiles

Evening:
  7. Log all applications made today against each talent
  8. Update statuses (who got a reply, who got an interview)
  9. Send notifications to talent whose status changed
```

## 1.7 What We Are NOT (Scope Boundaries)

```
❌ We are NOT a job board
   → Talent cannot browse and apply themselves

❌ We are NOT a freelance marketplace
   → Startups cannot directly message or hire talent on the platform

❌ We are NOT a payroll service
   → We do not process talent payments. Startup pays talent directly.

❌ We do NOT guarantee placement
   → We guarantee effort (X applications per month per plan)

✅ We ARE a done-for-you outreach & placement service
✅ We ARE the recruiter, working on behalf of the talent
```

## 1.8 Scale & Market

### Target Market Size (simplified, no fluff)

```
India has ~5 million software engineers (2024 estimate)
  └── ~500,000 actively looking for remote foreign work
        └── ~50,000 willing to pay for help with job search
              └── Even 1% of that = 500 paying customers
                    At ₹999 avg = ₹4,99,500/mo (~$6,000 MRR)
```

### Growth Path

```
Phase 1 — MVP (Month 1–3)
  Goal: 100 signups, 25 paying, first 5 placements
  Team: Founder + 1 placement manager
  Manual everything

Phase 2 — Stabilize (Month 4–6)
  Goal: 300 signups, 80 paying, 20 placements
  Team: +1 placement manager
  Semi-automate startup sourcing (Crunchbase API)

Phase 3 — Scale (Month 7–12)
  Goal: 1000 signups, 250 paying
  Add startup-facing portal
  Add referral system
  Explore DevOps-specific startup targeting (Series A+)
```
